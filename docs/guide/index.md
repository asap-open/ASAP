# Introduction

Welcome to ASAP (Applied Strength & Advancement Platform) - a comprehensive workout tracking and analytics system designed for serious lifters.

## What is ASAP?

ASAP is a **self-hosted, data-driven platform** that helps you track, analyze, and optimize your strength training. Unlike generic fitness apps, ASAP focuses specifically on the metrics that matter for building strength:

- **Precise workout logging** - Sets, reps, weight, and rest times
- **Progressive overload tracking** - Monitor volume and intensity trends
- **Body composition** - Track weight changes alongside performance
- **Exercise library** - Comprehensive database of exercises by muscle group
- **Historical analysis** - View your complete training history

## Why Self-Hosted?

**Privacy**: Your training data stays on your infrastructure
**Control**: Full ownership and customization
**Reliability**: No dependency on third-party services
**Longevity**: Your data persists regardless of company decisions

## Technology Stack

ASAP is built with modern, battle-tested technologies:

- **Frontend**: React + TypeScript + Vite
- **Backend**: Node.js + Express + TypeScript
- **Database**: PostgreSQL + Prisma ORM
- **Deployment**: Docker + Docker Compose
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS

## Philosophy

ASAP is built on the principle that **consistent, measured improvement** is the key to long-term strength gains. The platform emphasizes:

1. **Data Precision** - Accurate logging of every metric
2. **Long-term Tracking** - Historical data to identify trends
3. **Progressive Overload** - Structured progression over time
4. **Actionable Insights** - Clear visualization of what works

Whether you're a beginner or advanced lifter, ASAP provides the tools to make informed training decisions based on your actual data, not guesswork.
